# Laurel

> **Stub.** Created from the *Hollow's Last Hope* "Adventure
> Background" extract (`module:hlh:01-adventure-background`,
> decision D-005). Expand as more sourcebook content arrives.
>
> **First named NPC encountered in any extract import.**

A canny **local herbalist** in **Falcon's Hollow**. Knowledgeable,
methodical, and the first townsperson to identify the
**blackscour** contamination at Brookman's Well as the source of
the *wheezing death* outbreak.

## Narrative role in PathSeeker

- **Catalyst NPC** for the chapter-2 blackscour arc (per
  `module:hollow-last-hope`).
- Identifies the disease source.
- Attempts treatment with available herbs but lacks reagents for
  the final cure.
- Pleads / contracts with the **heroes NPCs** (per the
  secondary-PC framing D-009) and possibly Gherman alongside
  them, to retrieve cure reagents from Darkmoon Vale.
