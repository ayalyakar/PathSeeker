# Blackscour

> **Stub.** Created from the *Hollow's Last Hope* "Adventure
> Background" extract (`module:hlh:01-adventure-background`).
> Full mechanical stat block awaits later module-content import.

A **rare fungal disease**. The pathogen is a fungus that
contaminates water sources (in *Hollow's Last Hope*, Brookman's
Well in Falcon's Hollow). Inhalation or ingestion causes a
**hacking affliction** that progresses to fatal respiratory
failure — the **"wheezing death"** is the folk-name townsfolk use
for the late stage.

## Symptoms

- **Hacking cough** (early).
- **Wheezing**, progressive respiratory distress (mid).
- **Fatal respiratory failure** if untreated (late stage —
  *wheezing death*).

## Cure

Brewed by the herbalist Laurel from reagents found in **Darkmoon
Vale**. Specific reagents not yet imported (await later HLH
extract). Local remedies and low-rank divine healing (prayer at
Church of Iomedae) **fail to cure** it — see deferred decision
D-003 in `module:hlh:01-adventure-background` for the framing of
*why* divine healing fails (local-capacity vs disease-resistance
vs both).
