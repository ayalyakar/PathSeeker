# Falcon's Hollow

> **Stub.** Created from the *Hollow's Last Hope* "Adventure
> Background" extract (`module:hlh:01-adventure-background`). To
> be expanded as more sourcebook content is imported.

A frontier town in **Darkmoon Vale**, in the nation of **Andoran**,
on the **Foam River**. Run (in canon) by the Lumber Consortium —
TBD whether to preserve, rework, or replace.

## Narrative role in PathSeeker

- **Opening location** for the protagonist Gherman (per *Vision*
  §4.2 / §4.3).
- Gherman is found *seemingly dead* on the **Foam River shore**
  near Falcon's Hollow and brought into town to recover.
- He wakes up at the **Church of Iomedae** (per the
  `module:hlh:01-adventure-background` decision D-004 — relocated
  from the earlier Sarenrae-chapel resolution).
- Chapter 1 of the protagonist arc is a slice-of-life onboarding
  in Falcon's Hollow at peace.
- Chapter 2 onwards: the **blackscour outbreak** (per
  `module:hollow-last-hope`) triggers, kicking off the first
  major event arc.

## Notable sites

### The Church of Iomedae (Falcon's Hollow)

See *Lore - Faction - Church of Iomedae (Falcon's Hollow)*. The
local chapter of Iomedae's faith. Modest size; staffed by clerics
of low-to-middling rank. Where Gherman wakes up.

### Brookman's Well

`id: lore:falcons-hollow:brookmans-well`

A small spring on the edge of town. Source of the **blackscour**
contamination that triggers the disease outbreak (per
`module:hollow-last-hope`). The town constabulary bans its use
once the contamination is identified by Laurel.

### The Foam River shore (near town)

`id: lore:falcons-hollow:foam-river-shore`

Where Gherman is found seemingly dead (per *Vision* §4.2). Exact
spot, condition of his arrival, and witness identities pending
later design.

### Lumber Consortium presence

`id: lore:falcons-hollow:lumber-consortium`

Canonical PF1e: the Lumber Consortium effectively runs Falcon's
Hollow as a company-town. Their presence weighs on town politics,
crime, and economy.

**Status:** stub. Whether to preserve, rework, or replace the
