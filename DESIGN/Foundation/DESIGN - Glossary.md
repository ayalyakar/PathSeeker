# DESIGN — Glossary

> Living document. Project-internal terminology, abbreviations, and
> in-house words. When a term is used in multiple docs with project-
> specific meaning, define it here.

---

## 1. Conventions

- Entries alphabetized.
- Each entry: term, definition, cross-doc references.
- Mark **(canonical PF2e)** when a term has the upstream PF2e meaning.
- Mark **(reworked)** when we've changed an upstream term's meaning.
- Mark **(project)** when the term is invented by us.

## 2. Glossary (seed; expand as we go)

- **Anime Story Basis** **(project)** — *Vision* §7 pillar. The
  project's main focus: produce a living world whose emergent play
  reads as anime.
- **AP** **(canonical)** — Adventure Path.
- **Backlog** **(project)** — actionable-task list, lives in `Backlog/`;
  autonomously edited by the assistant per *Workflow* §1.1.
- **Body state** **(project)** — see *Body and Identity*.
- **Canonical body** **(project)** — see *NSFW* §5.1; the fixed-by-us
  baseline body.
- **Canonical tier** **(project)** — see *Content Pipeline*.
- **Common is common** **(project shorthand)** — *Vision* V-Q6
  resolution; Common as a starting language is accepted by setting
  convention without bespoke origin mechanism.
- **Content extract** **(project)** — a piece of source material being
  imported.
- **DESIGN folder** **(project)** — `DESIGN/` at project root, split
  into 6 sub-folders (Foundation, Systems, World, People, Tech, Art).
- **Diegetic** **(industry)** — UI/audio that exists within the fiction.
- **Edicts / Anathema** **(canonical)** — what a deity requires /
  forbids.
- **Essence-tier reagents** **(project)** — see *Magic* §1.1.
- **Falcon's Hollow** **(canonical)** — frontier town in Andoran's
  Darkmoon Vale; opening location (per *Vision* §4.2 / §4.3).
- **Forever project** **(project)** — see *Vision* §6. Subordinate
  to *Anime Story Basis* where they conflict.
- **Foam River** **(canonical)** — river near Falcon's Hollow;
  Gherman is found seemingly dead on its shore.
- **Gherman** **(project)** — the protagonist (see *Vision* §4).
- **Honor Remaster** **(project shorthand)** — the stance of fully
  adopting Remaster RAW where it differs from prior editions
  (especially R-Q3 alignment removal).
- **Hot cache** **(project)** — in-memory game-side data layer (see
  *Tech and Engine* §3).
- **Iconic Heroes** **(canonical)** — Paizo's pre-built sample
  characters.
- **In medias res** **(industry)** — opening a story in the middle
  of action; Gherman's framing per *Vision* §4.2.
- **Isekai** **(industry: anime/manga)** — protagonist transported
  from another world; Gherman's framing per *Vision* §4.2.
- **L0 / L1 / L2 / L3 / L4** **(project)** — simulation tick layers
  (see *World Simulation* §3).
- **LR-001** through **LR-004** **(project)** — confirmed lore
  reworks. See *Lore Reworks* §2.
- **MAP** **(canonical)** — Multiple Attack Penalty.
- **Modern outfit** **(project)** — anachronistic clothing Gherman
  wears at game start, alien to Golarion's medieval-fantasy
  aesthetic. See *Vision* §4.4.
- **NSFW asset filter** **(project)** — see *NSFW* §6 + N-Q13.
- **Open Question** **(project)** — see *Workflow* §5.
- **PF2e** — Pathfinder Second Edition.
- **PF2e Remaster** — the 2023+ rules update under ORC.
- **Plugin** **(project)** — see *Modding and Extensibility*.
- **Project rework** **(project)** — see *Lore Reworks*.
- **RAW** — rules as written.
- **Reagent** **(reworked)** — see *Magic* §1.1; required for casting
  in our Golarion.
- **Remaster** — short for PF2e Remaster.
- **Sarenrae** **(canonical)** — goddess of the sun, healing,
  redemption; first divine encounter for Gherman per *Vision* §4.5.
- **Session-Start Ritual** **(project)** — see *Workflow* §11. Three
  questions asked at every session start: mode, last-session check,
  re-import cadence.
- **Source tier** **(project)** — see *Content Pipeline*.
- **Story-state body** **(project)** — see *NSFW* §5.1.
- **Tattoo `08`** **(project)** — distinctive mark on the inside of
  Gherman's right wrist; meaning canonically unknown. See
  *Vision* §4.3.
- **Wrongness** **(project)** — the diegetic "something's off about
  him" aura that NPCs sense around Gherman regardless of how he's
  dressed. See *Vision* §4.4.
