# DESIGN — Drugs and Substances

> Living document. Alcohol, narcotics, alchemical drugs, magical
> intoxicants, herbs, aphrodisiacs, addiction, withdrawal, and their
> legal/social/intimate dimensions.

---

## 1. Stance

Substances are **first-class** — not a side gimmick. They have:
- A **physiological effect** (buffs, debuffs, hallucinations,
  arousal, violence, calm).
- An **addiction risk** profile.
- A **withdrawal profile**.
- A **legal status** per region.
- A **social meaning** per culture / faction.
- A **religious dimension** (Cayden Cailean alcohol, Calistria
  aphrodisiacs, Norgorber poisons, Urgathoa substances).

## 2. Categories

- **Alcohol** (beer, wine, spirits — region-specific styles).
- **Tobacco / pipeweed**.
- **Pesh** (canonical Golarion drug).
- **Flayleaf, scour, shiver, zerk** (canonical drugs).
- **Aphrodisiacs** (alchemical, magical).
- **Hallucinogens** (mushroom, ritual).
- **Magical intoxicants** (potion of *modify memory*, etc.).
- **Combat drugs** (mutagens, stimulants).
- **Sedatives** (knockout drops, magical sleep).
- **Poisons** (lethal, paralytic, sedative — see *Crime*).
