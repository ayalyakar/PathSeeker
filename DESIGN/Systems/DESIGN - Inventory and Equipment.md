# DESIGN — Inventory and Equipment

> Living document. Bags, weight, encumbrance, equip slots, runes,
> clothing layering, intimate items, and the inventory UX.

---

## 1. Stance

Inventory must support PF2e RAW (bulk, runes, magic items) with
real-time first-person feel. Heavy bias toward diegetic storage
(actual bags, chests, body slots).

## 2. Slots and Layering

- **Weapon slots** (main, off-hand, ranged, sheathed).
- **Armor / clothing layered** — underwear, base, outer, accessories.
- **Worn items** (rings, amulets, belts, runes-on-implements).
- **Carried** (bags, packs, pouches).
- **Stored** (chests, banks, workshops).
- **Mounted/vehicle storage**.

Clothing layering is critical for *NSFW*, *Body and Identity*,
*Crime and Justice* (disguise), *Religion* (vestments).

## 3. Bulk and Encumbrance (PF2e RAW)

Preserved with diegetic feedback (movement slows, breathing audio
heavier, animation changes).

## 4. Runes and Magic Items

Per Remaster: runes on weapons/armor; magic items in slots; rules
preserved. Rational-magic rework affects flavor (per *Magic*) but
not numbers.

## 5. Cross-References

- *Crafting* — produces items.
- *Magic* — magical items, runes.
- *Body and Identity* — worn items affect body.
- *NSFW* — intimate items, clothing as scene element.
- *Travel* — carried weight slows travel.
- *Economy* — buy/sell.
