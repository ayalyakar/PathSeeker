---

## 1. Stance

The economy is a **simulated system**, not a static price list:
- Regional prices vary and shift.
- Wars, plagues, weather, and rumors move markets.
- The player can **earn** in many ways (work, crafting, adventuring,
  sex work, performing, smuggling, gambling, investing, owning).
- The player can **own** (workshops, inns, brothels, ships, farms,
  caravans).
- **PF2e treasure-by-level** is one input but not the whole picture.

Currency: gold/silver/copper as PF2e RAW; regional currencies and
exchange rates may be modeled (per EC-Q below).

## 2. Player-Facing Surfaces

- **Wages** — earnable per-day at trades.
- **Markets** — buy/sell with regional spreads.
- **Investments** — fund expeditions, lend, partner.
- **Property** — own and run.
- **Taxes / tariffs** — region-dependent, faction-dependent.
- **Wealth display** — diegetic (purse, bank notes) vs UI.
