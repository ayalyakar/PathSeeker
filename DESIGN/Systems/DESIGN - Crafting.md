## 1. Stance

Crafting is a **major progression surface** (per *Core Loop and
Structure* §7), treated with AP-grade depth.

Crafts cover:

- **Smithing** (weapons, armor, tools, utility metalwork).
- **Alchemy** (potions, elixirs, bombs, mutagens, contraceptives,
  poisons, drugs, reagents).
- **Scribing** (scrolls, books, runes, glyphs, magical sigils,
  legal documents).
- **Tailoring / Leatherworking** (clothing, armor, harnesses,
  accessories, fashion).
- **Cooking / Brewing / Distilling** (food, drink, beer, wine,
  spirits, festival fare).
- **Jewelry** (rings, amulets, body jewelry, magical foci).
- **Carpentry / Stonework** (furniture, structures, mounts and
  carriages, ships).
- **Body-mod crafts** (tattooing, scarification, piercing,
  branding).
- **Sex-toy / intimate-item crafting** (per *NSFW*).
- **Magic-item enchantment** (under the alchemical/rational
  rework).
- **Engineering / Tinkering** (clockwork, traps, gunsmithing).
- **Cultivation** (alchemical herb farming, animal husbandry,
  reagent gathering).

## 2. Workshop, Tools, Materials

- A workshop is a **place** (owned, rented, employed-at) with tools
  and storage.
- Tools have quality tiers; better tools enable harder recipes.
- Materials must be **sourced** (gathered, bought, looted, traded).

## 3. Mastery

- PF2e proficiency tiers (untrained → legendary) per craft.
- Mastery unlocks recipes, reduces failure, enables exceptional
  outcomes.
- Apprenticeship: learn under an NPC master (per *Quests*).
