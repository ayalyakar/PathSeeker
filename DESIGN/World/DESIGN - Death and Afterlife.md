# DESIGN — Death and Afterlife

> Stub. Pharasma's judgment, soul mechanics, returning from the dead,
> resurrection costs and consequences, ghost/undead/lich paths,
> reincarnation. Tied to *Religion and Devotion*, *Lore Reworks*,
> *Planar Travel*, and the protagonist's narrative quasi-immortality
> (L-Q4 §6.1).

## Stance (proposed)

Death is **narrative for Gherman** (per L-Q4) but **real for
companions, NPCs, and antagonists**. The cosmological rework
(LR-Q6) determines how souls are sorted without alignment.

Returning from the dead exists per PF2e RAW (raise dead, resurrect,
clone, *true resurrection*) with full diegetic cost (rituals, gold,
days, faction implication).
