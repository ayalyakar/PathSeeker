# DESIGN — Planar Travel

> Stub. The Great Beyond, the Outer Sphere, the Inner Sphere, the
> Shadow Plane, the Astral / Ethereal, demi-planes. Becomes load-
> bearing for high-level play (Wrath of the Righteous, Strange Aeons,
> any planar AP).

## Stance (proposed)

Each plane is a **separate worldspace** (per LW-Q19) with its own
weather, ecology, NPC pool, and rules. The interplanar movement
system mirrors and extends overland travel.

The cosmology rework (per LR-Q5–LR-Q8) drives what planes exist and
how they're grouped.
