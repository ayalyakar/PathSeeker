## 1. Stance

In our Golarion:
- **Deities exist as empirically-detectable entities.** They have
  power signatures; their interventions are observable.
- **Worship is a relationship**, often transactional but not only:
  faith, love, reverence, contract, fear, trade.
- **Edicts and anathema** (per Remaster) are the operative axes —
  not alignment.
- **Divine magic is not different in kind** from arcane/primal/occult
  under the rational-magic rework, but its **source** is the deity
  contract.
- **Observance is a daily activity** — services, fasts, holy days,
  pilgrimage, prayer, sacred sex, sacred suffering, sacred art,
  sacred secrecy, sacred war.

## 2. Pantheon Coverage

The full canonical Golarion pantheon, plus minor deities, demigods,
empyreal lords, archdevils, demon lords, etc.

## 3. Personal Devotion (player and NPCs)

A devotee carries:
- A **patron / patrons** (multi-patron worship is allowed in
  Golarion).
- An **observance score** (rituals attended, edicts honored,
  anathema avoided).
- A **standing** with the deity (favored, warded, chosen, ignored,
  lapsed, anathema, struck).
- Access to **divine magic** (clerics, champions, divine sorcerers,
  oracles, animists) gated by observance and standing.
