# DESIGN — Tournaments and Festivals

> Stub. Holy days, tournaments, fairs, races, dueling clubs, drinking
> contests, religious processions, mass orgies, masquerades, executions
> as public events. Modeled as **first-class scheduled events** in the
> *World Simulation*.

## Stance (proposed)

Events are scheduled into the calendar (per *World Simulation* and
the Absalom Reckoning), drawing crowds, generating quests, providing
unique gameplay (gambling, contests, romantic encounters, set-piece
spectacles).
