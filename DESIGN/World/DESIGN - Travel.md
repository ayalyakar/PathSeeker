# DESIGN — Travel

> Living document. Overland, sea, planar travel; mounts, vehicles,
> caravans; the time-and-distance economics; companion logistics.

---

## 1. Stance

Travel is **continuously simulated** by default (per L-Q2 pending),
with optional time-skip for safe known routes. The 1:7.5 ratio means
"realistic" travel times are real-time meaningful (Absalom to
Magnimar takes weeks of real-time at 1:7.5 — we'll need policy here).

## 2. Modes

- **On foot**.
- **Mount** (horse, exotic) — *Mounts and Animals*.
- **Carriage / Wagon**.
- **Caravan** — multi-NPC organized travel.
- **Ship / Boat** — *Naval and Aerial*.
- **Flight** — magical mounts, aerial animals, magical flight.
- **Magical** (teleport, *plane shift*, *transposition*, etc.).

## 3. Travel-Layer Encounters

While traveling, the simulation rolls for:
- Random encounters (PF2e tables + originals).
- Weather events.
- NPC meetings on the road.
- Travel-related quest hooks.

## 4. Companions and Travel

- Companions agree to travel; gear up; manage their own resources.
- Disagreements during travel can split a party (per *NPCs and
  Companions*).
