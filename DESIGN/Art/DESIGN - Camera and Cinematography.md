# DESIGN — Camera and Cinematography

> Living document. Camera rules: first-person free-look default,
> locked / framed dialogue and scene cameras, NSFW scene direction.
> Pulled out of *Art and Presentation* §3 because scene framing is its
> own craft.

---

## 1. Stance (recap and extend)

- **First-person free-look** is default (Q27).
- **Camera-locked / framed** for dialogue, cinematics, NSFW scenes,
  rituals, executions, key beats.
- **Smooth transitions** between modes are non-negotiable.
- **Cinematography language**: scenes are *directed*, not just
  rendered.

## 2. Modes

| Mode | When | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| FP free-look | Default exploration / casual interaction | Player-controlled |
| FP locked-pose | Mounted, sitting, intimate set-pieces | Player retains head/torso freedom |
| Over-the-shoulder framed | Dialogue, some scenes | Scene-driven, smooth |
| Cinematic | Cutscenes, scene transitions | Director-controlled, multi-shot |
| Scene-direct | NSFW scenes, rituals | Multi-angle, scene-aware framing |
| Photo / mirror | Cosmetic check, photo mode (A-Q8) | Player-paused |
