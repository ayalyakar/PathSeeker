# DESIGN — Property and Businesses

> Stub. Owning land, buildings, businesses, kingdoms; running them;
> employing NPCs; collecting income. Loadbearing for Kingmaker
> (kingdom-tier) and any settled-life play.

## Stance (proposed)

Player can own from a **room at the inn** up to a **kingdom**,
with progressive depth at each tier. Properties have:
- A location.
- Maintenance and improvement costs.
- Income and expenses.
- Employees (NPCs).
- Customers / subjects (NPCs / aggregate).
- Reputation effects.
