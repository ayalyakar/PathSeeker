# DESIGN — Politics and Intrigue

> Stub. Court politics, noble houses, intrigue, conspiracies,
> assassinations, succession, marriages-of-state. Load-bearing for
> Curse of the Crimson Throne, War for the Crown, Hell's Vengeance,
> Council of Thieves, and any nobility-focused play.

## Stance (proposed)

Politics is **a faction-graph subset** (per *Factions and
Reputation*) plus **per-house anatomy** for noble houses, plus
**intrigue mechanics** (rumors, blackmail, alliances, spies) running
on the *World Simulation* tick.
