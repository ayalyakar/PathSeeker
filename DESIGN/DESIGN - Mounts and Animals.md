## Stance (proposed)

Animals are NPC-architecture-light (per NPC-Q40), with full
behavior, training, bonding, breeding, ageing, and death.

Player mounts are first-class travel agents (per *Travel*).
Familiars are PF2e RAW (sustained as the spell describes).
